# Paid Services

This free invoice generator can be customized for freelancers, agencies, consultants, and solo service businesses.

## Service packages

### Quick setup - $29

Best for freelancers who want a simple branded version.

Includes:

- Business name and footer text update
- Brand color adjustment
- Payment instruction wording
- Basic invoice field cleanup

### Business invoice template - $99

Best for service businesses that need a more specific invoice format.

Includes:

- Custom invoice fields
- Branded layout adjustments
- Payment terms and late payment wording
- Tax or discount display adjustments
- One revision round

### Client-ready mini tool - $199

Best for a freelancer, agency, or consultant who wants a public invoice tool page for their own brand.

Includes:

- Custom landing copy
- Custom color and typography direction
- Invoice fields tailored to the business
- GitHub Pages deployment support
- Two revision rounds
